Curriculum Vitae
Research Interests

Justin Sirignano's research interests include machine learning, optimization, stochastics, and finance. Recent projects include machine learning models for limit order books and loan risk. He won the 2014 SIAM Financial Mathematics and Engineering Conference Paper Prize. Justin Sirignano will be a Chapman Fellow at the Department of Mathematics, Imperial College London for 2015-2016. In Fall 2016, he will join University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign as an Assistant Professor (tenure track).
